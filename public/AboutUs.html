About Us

Welcome to Usena, where friendship meets innovation! We are a team of passionate individuals committed to making university life easier by connecting students with essential resources like housing and second-hand products.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create an easy-to-use platform for university students to buy, sell, and rent items, as well as find suitable housing arrangements. We're here to help students save money and make their daily life more convenient.

Our Vision

We envision a community where students are empowered by technology to connect, share, and collaborate effortlessly, helping each other make the most of their university experience.
